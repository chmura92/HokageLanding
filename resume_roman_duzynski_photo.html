Approach

Before writing any code, I run event storming sessions with domain experts to surface the actual domain model — not just the stated requirements.

I default to a modular monolith and add distribution only when team size or operational load makes it necessary.

Having worked directly with clients for years, I factor business constraints and delivery risk into every architectural decision, not as an afterthought.

I use Claude Code to tighten the loop between design and working code — it lets me prototype and validate faster without adding overhead.

I care about making the right call, shipping it cleanly, and leaving the codebase in better shape than I found it.
